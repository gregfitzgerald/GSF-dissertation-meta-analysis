Rodent Sex Subgroup Comparison

Note. Forest plot comparing effect sizes between male-only (k = 47 studies) and female-only (k = 17 studies) samples. Male studies showed g = 0.57, 95% CI [0.35, 0.79]; female studies showed g = 0.49, 95% CI [0.26, 0.73]. The difference was not significant (z = 0.48, p = .633). Studies using both sexes (k = 6) were excluded. Analysis uses three-level meta-analytic model with robust variance estimation.
