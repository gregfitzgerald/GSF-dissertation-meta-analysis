Rodent Species Subgroup Comparison (Rat vs. Mouse)

Note. Forest plot comparing effect sizes between rat (k = 23 studies) and mouse (k = 48 studies) samples. Rat studies showed g = 0.50, 95% CI [0.24, 0.77]; mouse studies showed g = 0.40, 95% CI [0.15, 0.65]. The difference was not significant (z = 0.58, p = .562). Analysis uses three-level meta-analytic model with robust variance estimation.
